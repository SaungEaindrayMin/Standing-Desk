Health Benefits

Using a height-adjustable desk to go from sitting to standing is a scientifically proven method to improve your lifestyle and increase your awareness of the moment throughout the day.

Using a sit-stand workstation has been shown to significantly reduce lower back pain.

A standing desk allows you to improve your posture by training your back to stay straight with your shoulders pulled back.

The CDC found that standing an hour a day at work can reduce upper back and neck pain by more than half.

Moving enhances the creation of new brain cells, improving critical thinking and focus.

A height-adjustable desk allows you to keep your arms at a 90-degree angle and your wrists level, reducing arm pain and putting less strain on your wrists.
